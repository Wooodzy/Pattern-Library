Fluid jumbotron.

This is a modified jumbotron that occupies the entire horizontal space of its parent.

Fluid Jumbotron Elements

"jumbotron" Is an attention grabbing element that is usually at the top of the page or the bottom to draw users in, it usually has large text and a call to action button.

<div class= "jumbotron jumbotron-fluid" >
<h3 class= "display-2" >Fluid jumbotron. </h3>
<div class= "jumbotron jumbotron-fluid" >
<p class= "lead" > </p>
<button class= "btn fill yellow" >Sign up Today </button>
</div>
</div>

Jumbotron Call to Action

The call to action aspect of a jumbotron can be anything from social media links to a subcribe button for the user to interact with aslong as it gets the users attention.
